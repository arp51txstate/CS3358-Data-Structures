Proj 6 The Towers of Hanoi and Recursion

Given n discs: disc1, disc2, ... , discn ; stacked on the leftmost peg in 
order of size with the largest disc at the bottom, and each disc is always 
smaller than the disc below it,

Move all n discs from leftmost peg A to rightmost peg C with the following 
rules: 

1.) Only one disc may be moved at a time.
2.) A disc cannot be placed on the top of a smaller disc.
3.) There are only three pegs: A,B,C.

Write the program to display every move and indicate the position of each 
disc. Do this for n = 3,4,5.
